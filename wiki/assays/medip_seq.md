# MeDIP-seq

> 196 datasets | 2015/01/01 – 2026/04/08

Methylated DNA Immunoprecipitation sequencing (MeDIP-seq). Uses an antibody against 5-methylcytosine to immunoprecipitate methylated DNA fragments before sequencing. Provides genome-wide methylation enrichment data but at lower resolution than bisulfite-based methods.
