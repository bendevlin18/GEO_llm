# RRBS (Reduced Representation Bisulfite Sequencing)

> 516 datasets | 2015/01/01 – 2026/04/08

Reduced Representation Bisulfite Sequencing (RRBS). A cost-efficient bisulfite sequencing approach that focuses coverage on CpG-rich regions by using restriction enzyme digestion (typically MspI) prior to sequencing. Provides single-base resolution at a fraction of the cost of WGBS.
