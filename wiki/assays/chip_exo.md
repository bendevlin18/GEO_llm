# ChIP-exo

> 87 datasets | 2015/01/01 – 2026/04/08

ChIP-exo adds an exonuclease digestion step to standard ChIP-seq, providing single-nucleotide resolution of protein-DNA binding. Primarily used for precise TF binding site mapping.
