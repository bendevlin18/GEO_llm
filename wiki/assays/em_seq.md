# EM-seq (Enzymatic Methyl-seq)

> 59 datasets | 2015/01/01 – 2026/04/08

Enzymatic Methyl-seq (EM-seq) uses TET2 and APOBEC3A enzymes instead of bisulfite conversion to distinguish methylated from unmethylated cytosines. Preserves DNA integrity better than bisulfite treatment, enabling higher complexity libraries and more uniform coverage.
