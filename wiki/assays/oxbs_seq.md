# oxBS-seq / TAB-seq

> 40 datasets | 2015/01/01 – 2026/04/08

Oxidative Bisulfite Sequencing (oxBS-seq) uses potassium perruthenate to oxidize 5hmC to 5fC before bisulfite conversion, allowing strand-specific 5mC quantification at single-base resolution. TET-Assisted Bisulfite sequencing (TAB-seq) is a related approach for profiling 5hmC directly.
