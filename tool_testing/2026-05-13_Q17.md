### Highlights & Notes

- 🏆 **Largest pure-rat datasets:** **GSE74728** (n=95, neurotoxicology), **GSE75772** (n=79, brain aging/cognition), **GSE56743** (n=125, kidney tubules — richest processed data with RPKM tables), and **GSE78702** (n=58, skeletal muscle aging).
- 🧠 **Neuroscience-focused:** GSE75772, GSE63309 (pineal gland), GSE57110 (calorie restriction/neuroprotection), GSE60752 (ischemia), GSE64815 (hypothalamus/hippocampus), GSE75402/GSE75120 (epilepsy models).
- 🫀 **Organ/tissue-specific highlights:** Kidney (GSE56743), Liver/HBV (GSE68112), Uterus (GSE69819, GSE68542), Bone (GSE67787), Skeletal muscle aging (GSE78702).
- ✅ **Processed data available:** GSE56743 (RPKM.txt.gz), GSE75772 (DEseq counts), GSE78702 (count matrix), GSE65715 (raw counts), GSE75402/GSE75120 (count matrices), GSE68112 (counts + RPKM).
- ⚠️ **Multi-species datasets** (GSE61991, GSE43013, GSE43013) include rat data but are not rat-exclusive — verify which samples correspond to *R. norvegicus* before downloading.
