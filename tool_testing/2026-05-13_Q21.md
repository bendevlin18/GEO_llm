### Best Matches & Notes

**Top picks for straightforward hippocampal cell-type atlas work:**
- **GSE98679** (n=912) and **GSE85721** (n=924) are the largest and most established mouse hippocampus snRNA-seq datasets, with flat processed count/TPM files that are immediately analysis-ready. GSE98679 specifically resolves dentate gyrus granule neuron activity states. These two are well-matched in design and could be used together for cross-study validation.
- **GSE104525** (DroNc-seq, n=10) is a landmark droplet-based snRNA-seq dataset from mouse hippocampus/cortex with a clean UMI count matrix.

**Disease/perturbation models with ≥6 samples:**
- **GSE295077**, **GSE302153** — 5XFAD Alzheimer's
