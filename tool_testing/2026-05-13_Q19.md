### Best Matches & Notes

- **[GSE196939](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE196939)** (28 samples) is the cleanest dedicated mouse liver ATAC-seq dataset — comparing C57BL/6J vs. 129S1/SvImJ strains under fed/fasted conditions, with well-organized bigWig and count matrix files.
- **[GSE143525](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE143525)** (32 samples) is excellent for circadian/feeding biology, profiling multiple liver cell types (hepatocytes, endothelial cells, Kupffer cells).
- **[GSE131853](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE131853)** / **[GSE131848](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE131848)** / **[GSE131852](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE131852)** are related SubSeries of a larger study on sex-biased chromatin and GH/STAT5 signaling in liver.
- **[GSE243630](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE243630)** (8 samples) is ideal if you are interested in **liver aging** chromatin dynamics.
- **[GSE246213](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE246213)** (12 samples) focuses on **NAFLD/MAFLD and hepatocellular carcinoma**.
- ⚠️ **GSE121913/GSE121915** are part of a SuperSeries on xenobiotic (TCPOBOP/CAR) response — note GSE121915 includes ChIP-seq samples mixed in.
- ⚠️ **GSE210779** focuses on *fetal liver* erythropoiesis, not adult hepatocyte biology.
- ⚠️ **GSE224277** includes humanized liver mice (human hepatocytes in a mouse background), so it's a mixed-species dataset.
