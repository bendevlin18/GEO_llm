### Best Matches & Notes

- 🏆 **GSE61224** is the most focused dataset — a canonical genome-wide chromatin state map (H3K4me3 + H3K27me3) specifically in pluripotent hESCs comparing primed vs. naive states. Ideal as a clean reference.
- 🏆 **GSE90680** is the most comprehensive single dataset (29 samples, 11.4 GB), covering multiple histone marks (H3K4me3, H3K27me3, H3K27ac, H3K4me1, H3K9me3) across naive and primed hESC states, with processed peak files readily available.
- **GSE159650** is excellent for developmental trajectory work — it includes H3K4me3 in hESCs tracked through mesoderm and primordial germ cell specification, with pre-processed narrowPeak files for each stage.
- **GSE182771** and **GSE122128** are useful if you're interested in hESC differentiation toward neural or ectodermal lineages.
- **GSE80086** is specifically designed for studying the effect of *JMJD3* (a H3K27me3 demethylase) on H3K4me3 landscapes — relevant for bivalency studies.

### ⚠️ Caveats
- Several datasets (GSE105258, GSE122128, GSE182771) include hESC samples but are **not exclusively hESC** — they cover differentiated derivatives too.
- **GSE135638** was excluded from the table as it is mouse ESC data, not human.
